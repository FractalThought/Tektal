# Introduktion till Listor

---

## Rubriker och paragrafer

```html
<h1>Rubrik här</h1>
<p>Detta är en paragraf</p>
```

---

## List-item

```html
<li>Spider-man</li>
```

---

```html
<h2>Characters</h2>
<li>Iron Man</li>
<li>Captain America</li>

<li>Superman</li>
<li>Batman</li>
```

## Characters

- Iron Man
- Captain America
- Superman
- Batman

---

## List-items behöver kapslas in

1. **OL:** Ordered list

- **UL:** Unordered list

---

```html
<ol>
  <li>Superman</li>
  <li>Wonder Woman</li>
  <li>Batman</li>
</ol>
<ul>
  <li>Iron Man</li>
  <li>Captain America</li>
  <li>Spider-Man</li>
</ul>
```

---

1. Superman
2. Wonder Woman
3. Batman

- Iron Man
- Captain America
- Spider-man
